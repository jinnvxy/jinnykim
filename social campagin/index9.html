Silence, Break it up - Poster

May 2025

This poster represents that bullying is not just physical or emotional harmful words but it is also whether you stay silent and ignore the scene without taking any actions. I decided to use the letter “i” to represent the person. The silence of the i is missing as it expresses that the silence slowly hurts the victims. The compelmentary colors are used to catch the attendance of the audience as it will help to standout more.
