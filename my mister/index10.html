My Mister - Websites

February 2025

My Mister tells the story of Ji-An, a young woman burdened by debt and loneliness, and Dong-Hoon, a worn-down middle-aged man navigating family and workplace struggles. Though separated by age and circumstance, they form a quiet bond that becomes a source of healing. This project is inspired by the drama’s restrained yet powerful language — words that do not try to save, but simply stay. Each page centers on a line from the series, whether iconic or personally comforting, translating dialogue into a visual space where viewers can pause, reflect, and feel understood. Through these fragments, I invite others to experience the same solace I found.
