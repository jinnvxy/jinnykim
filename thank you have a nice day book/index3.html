Thank you, Have a Nice Day - Book

December 2025

We say “thank you” every day, often without noticing its meaning. This project explores the quiet power of gratitude hidden inside ordinary moments. By reimagining the receipt as a record of appreciation, each page becomes a tangible trace of what we receive from life — a smile, a shared meal, a brief softness in an otherwise routine day. These fragments are collected into a 100-page book that functions as an archive of everyday thank-yous. Rather than celebrating grand gestures, the work honors the unnoticed exchanges that shape our lives, reminding us that even the smallest moment of gratitude is proof that something mattered.
